Keep Your Customers Coming Back

Membership system has never been so simple.
Discard let businesses create and manage members right from your smartphone. Highly customizable to serve all purposes. Get to know your customers better without adding thickness to their wallet. No extra hardware required.

Membership

Make your customer feel special. Create, search and manage your members at your fingertips. Members can signup themselves by downloading the app or you can add them manually. You also have a choice to search and validate the members by scanning their unique QR code or search for them by email or phone number. So, whether you own a brick and mortar, delivery or online virtual business, Discard serves all purposes.

Rewards

With our stamp collection systems, you can setup a rewards campaign to keep your customers coming back for more. Using a simple single tap on your device, you can give out stamps and give away the rewards, while your customers get real-time update on their device. Our stamps are customizable, just like you make your own physical rubber stamp; meaning you can be unique, making your customers remember the experience.
